Data Engineer
Responsibility
· Developing systems that collect, manage, and convert raw data into usable information for data scientists and business analysts to interpret
· Making data accessible so that organizations can use it to evaluate and optimize their performance
· Transforming source data and target data by ETL tool as well as transform target data to source data.
· Using programming language and tools, identify ways to improve data reliability, efficiency and quality
· Ensuring data management and governance to maintain the data integrity.

·      SSIS
·      Microsoft Azure
·      AWS
·      Google Cloud Platform
·      Other

·      Understanding analyzing complex database query
·      Experience in processing ETL design and development standards and techniques
·      Good knowledge of ETL tools (Extract, Transform and Load)
·      Good knowledge of Hadoop (HDFS, YARN, HBase, Sqoop)
·      Good knowledge of Azure, AWS, GCP cloud data platform
·      Very good analytical, problem-solving and interpersonal skills
·      Good English communications and writing skills are a plus
